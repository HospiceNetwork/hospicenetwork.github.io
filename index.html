Our Services

Panel 1 Panel 2 Panel 3

A website that can connect different hospitals of a region, collect various datas from the hospitals and provide the informations of doctors, beds, medications, availability of blood/organs, etc to the end users. Present website have many similar features but requires a lot of improvement and needs some new add on features as seen from the reviews of the users.Some of the common health websites like apollo, practo, medlineplus, etc have features like video consultancy, drug delivery, lab test, medical records etc.

eHealth is the delivery of health care using modern electronic information and communication technologies when health care providers and patients are not directly in contact and their interaction is mediated by electronic means. Among other things, the services that are thus provided include physical and psychological diagnosis and treatment, telepathology, vital signs monitoring, electronic prescribing, teleconsultation, etc.

A e-health website is decided to be designed which will be joining the hospitals of a region , their datas ,etc. After going thorugh various E-health websites (eg .practo, apollo, etc. ) some topics have been found which can be enhanced and can be updated in our new website. When patient/end user will access the website the first thing he/she will need is to consult a doctor which can be done through OPD section provided in website.Further procedures as directed by the doctor can be accessed by patient /end user on his /her account.
